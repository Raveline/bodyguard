Bodyguard demo v0.2

Basic and short demo of a fairly simple game. Warning : still very buggy.
  • If you've already played and notice strange bugs, clear your browser cache.
  • Left click to move to somewhere on the map.
  • Right click to shoot. (Don't shoot your employer !)
  • Bored by the (ugly, I know) cutscenes ? Right click to skip any of them.
  • Beware : your employer may stop for a while randomly in his path. It's not a bug, it's a feature.
  • Beware 2 : don't shoot the guy you're supposed to meet till he becomes hostile, or you'll lose.
